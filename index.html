Frequently Asked Questions

Is ITR filing mandatory?

Yes, if your gross income exceeds the basic exemption limit (₹2.5L under the new regime) or if you have certain types of income or transactions that require mandatory filing, ITR filing is required by law.

How long does GST registration take?

Typically, GST registration is completed within 3–7 working days after all required documents are submitted. In some cases, it may take longer if additional verification is needed by the GST department.

Do you provide support after filing?

Absolutely. We provide complete post-filing support including assistance with notices, corrections, revised returns, and any queries from the income tax or GST department at no extra cost.

What documents are needed for ITR filing?

For salaried individuals: Form 16, bank statements, investment proofs, and Aadhaar/PAN. For business owners: P&L statement, balance sheet, bank statements, and GST returns. We'll guide you with a customized checklist.

Can you help with company registration from scratch?

Yes, we handle the entire process from name approval to certificate of incorporation, including PAN, TAN, GST registration, and opening a current bank account for your new company.
